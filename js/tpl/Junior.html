About Junior Cycle

The Junior Cycle comprises of 1st, 2nd & 3rd year and culminates in the Junior Certificate Examination held in June at the end if 3rd Year.

Causeway Comprehensive offers a broad curriculum at Junior Cycle. Students attend classes in 11 core subjects and have additional choice of 7 optional subjects.

Subjects (Core & Optional)

The subjects are.. Gaeilge, English, Mathematics, Religious Education, Science, C.S.P.E., French, S.P.H.E., History, Geography, Physical Education.

All Students take the core subjects and choose three from the optional list.
